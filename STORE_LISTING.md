## Full description

Investigation Flow is desktop video software built for one job: turning raw
surveillance footage into clean, defensible evidence, fast. It was made for
private investigators and surveillance professionals, not filmmakers, so there
is no bloated timeline and no learning curve. Just the four things your case
work actually needs, and nothing it does not.

General video editors were never designed for surveillance. Adding an accurate
date and time stamp is a known headache, and stitching a full day of clips
together by hand wastes hours. Investigation Flow fixes that.

What you can do:

- Add verifiable timestamps. Burn accurate date and time onto footage that no
  longer carries it, recovering the original recording time from the file's
  metadata so your timestamps hold up under scrutiny.
- Stitch a whole case into one sequence. Drop in clips from a day of
  surveillance and combine them into a single, logical, chronological video.
- Keep or remove audio. Toggle audio on or off per clip for privacy or
  admissibility, with no re-encoding headaches.
- Redact, highlight, annotate, and brand. Blur bystanders and license plates,
  spotlight your subject, add text or notes, and place your logo, right on the
  video.
- Grab clean still shots. Pull stills straight from the footage in one click,
  ready for reports, exhibits, and client updates.
- Generate a full action report. Every action is logged in order, from the
  moment footage is imported through each edit to the final export, giving you
  a clear, defensible record of exactly what was done to the video.
- Batch process entire folders. Handle a whole folder of clips in a single pass
  instead of one file at a time.
- Export a universal MP4. Render a clean, widely compatible MP4 in minutes,
  ready to hand to clients and drop into case files.

Fast by design. The conversion engine uses your computer's full hardware
encoding capability, so large batches finish in a fraction of the time.

Install in minutes and start working immediately. No editing degree required.
Download and try it free on your own footage.

## What's new in this version (1.3.0)

Faster conversions and cleaner effects.

Conversion engine overhaul:

- Dramatically faster video conversions, so large batches finish in a fraction
  of the time.
- Conversions now use your computer's full hardware encoding capability on
  every run.
- Merged exports are assembled directly, eliminating a wasteful second encoding
  pass.
- Adding blurs or spotlights no longer meaningfully slows down conversion.
- Fixed conversions getting progressively slower the longer the app was open.
- Faster exports on computers without hardware video encoders.

Timeline and effects fixes:

- Effects (blurs, spotlights, text) now clear from the timeline immediately
  after they are burned into an export. Previously they could linger until an
  app restart.
- New "Keep for all exports" option on picture overlays: keep it checked and
  your logo or badge re-applies to every export automatically, or uncheck it to
  burn the picture once and clear it like any other effect.
